About Us

Here at Spinnin' Records our goal is to share our love of music with the world. We appreciate all music, collecting all genres and languages of music from all decades so that everyone can find something they enjoy. We encourage everyone to visit our stores to stop by for a listening session and bond with fellow music lovers! With locations all around the globe, our outreach is only expanding.

Our Roots

Started by two best friends who bonded over their love of music, Paul Zim and Jess Rookey, Spinnin' Records began out of Paul's parents' garage in his childhood neighbourhood. Its local success lead to worldwide success, developing a community of likeminded music enthusiasts around the globe.
